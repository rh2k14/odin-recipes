Sugar Cookies

Description

A simple yet delicious cookie, the sugar cookie is the perfect cookie for everyone to enjoy, including Santa! Soft, sweet, and chewy: these sugar cookies are the perfect way to enjoy the joys of baking while not needing complicated techniques or equipment. With just some simple ingredients, some simple steps, and lots of love, anyone can make these cookies to enjoy by yourself or to share!

Ingredients

  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    •

    Steps

    1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
    2. Stir flour, baking soda, and baking powder together in a small bowl.
    3. Beat sugar and butter together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until smooth. Beat in egg and vanilla. Gradually blend in flour mixture. Roll dough into walnut-sized balls and place 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.
    4. Taste and adjust sweetness as needed.
    5. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are golden, 8 to 10 minutes. Cool on the baking sheets briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
